Ryde is a bicycle sharing platform that provides a convenient and eco-friendly mode of transportation for urban residents and tourists.

The company offers a variety of bikes for rent that can be picked up and dropped off at various locations around the city.

Users can access the bikes through the Ryde app, which allows them to locate and reserve bikes, track their ride, and make payments.

Ryde aims to promote sustainable transportation and reduce traffic congestion, making cities more livable and environmentally friendly.
